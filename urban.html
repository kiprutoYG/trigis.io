Tracing Urban Growth in Nairobi (2000-2020)

This is a comprehensive research project exploring the patterns and drivers of urban sprawl in Nairobi over the past two decades. Through geospatial mapping, statistical analysis, and qualitative research, it examines the expansion's impact on infrastructure, socio-economic factors, and environmental sustainability. The project aims to inform policymakers and stakeholders about the dynamics of urbanization, fostering dialogue for more sustainable and inclusive urban development strategies.
